About

Michael Pickin

Winning my first competition aged fourteen, I have participated in a number of public airshows and competitions throughout the UK.

As an Aerobatic Pilot, time keeping, safety, self-discipline and good judgement are all essential qualities for which I have been commended on having a number of times by organisers.
